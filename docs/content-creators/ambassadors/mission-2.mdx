# 🔍 Mission 2: RuneRealm Hidden Secrets
## *Uncover and Share the Magic of RuneRealm*

Congratulations on completing Mission 1! Now it's time to dive deeper into the RuneRealm universe and discover its hidden secrets. RuneRealm is filled with many unique features and hidden gems waiting to be discovered and shared with the community.

---

## 📋 Mission Brief

**Objective**: Explore RuneRealm, discover a unique or hidden feature that interests you, and create an engaging X Post with a visual element (preferably a moving graphic/GIF) to showcase your finding.

**Duration**: 10 days from acceptance

**Difficulty**: Intermediate

**Rewards**: 
- 1 RuneRealm Eternal Pass

---

## 🎯 Simple Steps to Complete

### 1. Find Something Cool (1-2 hours)
- [ ] Play RuneRealm at <a href={RUNEREALM.gameSite} target="_blank">the game site</a>
- [ ] Find a unique feature that interests you
- [ ] Take a screenshot or recording of it

### 2. Create a Visual (30 minutes)
- [ ] Make a GIF or image of your discovery
- [ ] Keep it simple but clear - show what you found!

### 3. Post on X (15 minutes)
- [ ] Write a short post about what you found
- [ ] Add your GIF/image
- [ ] Tag <a href={RUNEREALM.twitter} target="_blank">@RuneRealm</a> and use #RuneRealm

### 4. Engage (15 minutes)
- [ ] Share your post in <a href={ARCAO.ambassadorWins} target="_blank">#wins</a> Discord
- [ ] Reply to any comments you receive
- [ ] Check out what other Ambassadors discovered

---

## 📊 Success Criteria

Your mission is complete when:

1. You've posted about a unique RuneRealm feature
2. Your post includes a visual (GIF preferred)
3. You've tagged @RuneRealm and used #RuneRealm
4. Your post gets at least 5 engagements
5. You've shared it in Discord

---

## 📝 Submission

To complete this mission:

1. @mod
2. Include a link to your X Post
3. Add a short note about what you discovered (2-3 sentences)

That's it! A Community Manager will review and approve within 48 hours.

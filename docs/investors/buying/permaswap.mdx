# How to Buy $GAME on AO (Permaswap) 🎮

This guide explains how to acquire the **$GAME** token on the AO (Arweave) network using **Permaswap**. It walks you through bridging assets into AO and swapping AO/wUSDC for $GAME directly on Permaswap. 【167†source】

---

## 1. What Is Permaswap? 🧩

Permaswap is the first cross-chain decentralized exchange (DEX) built on Arweave and deployed on the AO network.  
It provides automated market maker (AMM) functionality for token swaps and liquidity provision, making it a core venue for token trading within AO. 【167†source】

## 3. Step-by-Step Guide to Buying $GAME with Permaswap 🪜

### Step 1: Bridge Assets into AO 🌉
Use [AOX](https://aox.xyz/#/home) to convert assets into AO-compatible tokens (**wUSDC, wAR**).

### Step 2: Visit Permaswap Swap Interface 🌐
![PermaSwap](/img/docs/investor/buying/IMGPermaswap/Picture1.png)  
Navigate to: [https://www.permaswap.network/#/tokens](https://www.permaswap.network/#/tokens).



### Step 3: Connect Your AO Wallet 🔗
![PermaSwap](/img/docs/investor/buying/IMGPermaswap/Picture2.png)
Use your Wander Wallet or another AO-supporting wallet to connect.


### Step 4: Setup the Swap ⚙️
Select AO/wUSDC as your source token and choose $GAME as the token to acquire.

### Step 5: Input Swap Amount & Execute 🎯
Enter the amount, review slippage and fees, then approve in your wallet.

### Step 6: Receive $GAME in Your Wallet 🎉
Upon confirmation, $GAME tokens will appear in your AO wallet. 【167†source】

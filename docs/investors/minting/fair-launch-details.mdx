### Distribution Rationale
- **Fair Launch (70%)** — The majority of tokens are distributed transparently to the community via a fair launch.
- **Team (15%)** — Reserved to support long-term protocol development and alignment.
- **Airdrops (5%)** — To incentivize early community engagement and active participation.
- **Strategic Partnerships (5%)** — For key integrations, co-marketing, and technical collaboration.
- **Liquidity Pairing (5%)** — Ensures token availability and price stability post-launch.

> **0% allocated to VCs.** Arc is a project truly for the builders and believers—not early private buyers.

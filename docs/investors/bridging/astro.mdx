# 🌉 How to Bridge USDC → USDA via Astro Bridge

You can use the official **Astro Bridge** interface to convert **USDC** into **USDA** on AO.  
Access it here: [https://bridge.astrousd.com/](https://bridge.astrousd.com/).

---

## 🔎 1. Understanding What USDA and the Astro Bridge Do

- 💵 **USDA (Astro USD)** is the native stablecoin of the AO (Arweave) ecosystem — a unified asset aggregating multiple stablecoins and fiat sources into one seamless layer.  
- 🌐 The **Astro Bridge** facilitates seamless conversion of stablecoins, including USDC, into USDA minted directly on AO via smart contracts.  
- 🔗 It supports bridging from multiple source networks and assets.

---

## 🛠 2. Step-by-Step: Bridging USDC to USDA

### 1️⃣ Step 1: Go to the Astro Bridge Interface
Visit [https://bridge.astrousd.com/](https://bridge.astrousd.com/) to access the live bridge tool.

![Astro Bridge Homepage](/img/docs/investor/bridging/astro/Picture1.png)

### 2️⃣ Step 2: Connect Your Wallets
- 🔑 Connect your **Wander Wallet** to receive USDA on AO.  
- 💼 Connect **MetaMask** (or another compatible wallet) to lock USDC from supported networks (Ethereum, Base, Polygon, or Solana).

![Connect Wallets](/img/docs/investor/bridging/astro/Picture2.png)

### 3️⃣ Step 3: Select the Source Chain & Stablecoin
Choose the blockchain where your USDC is held (Ethereum, Base, Polygon, or Solana). Then select **USDC** as the stablecoin to bridge.

### 4️⃣ Step 4: Enter the Amount
Input the amount of USDC you wish to bridge (minimum ~$5). The interface shows a real-time estimate of how much USDA you’ll receive.

### 5️⃣ Step 5: Confirm Transaction Details
Review all details: source chain, stablecoin, USDA estimate, and expected processing time.

### 6️⃣ Step 6: Approve the Transaction on MetaMask
Approve the transaction in MetaMask (or another wallet). Wait for confirmations before proceeding.

### 7️⃣ Step 7: Receive USDA in Wander Wallet
Once confirmed, **USDA** is automatically minted and delivered to your Wander Wallet on AO.
